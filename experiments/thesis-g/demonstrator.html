Interdependency, read three ways experimental · not canonical

A sandbox demonstrator for thesis G (PMESII interdependency), the highest-false-precision-risk thesis. One Meridian-derived graph, three reads — finding the rung where honesty ends. Meridian is fiction (vignette §8); nothing here touches core ASSAY. See thesis-g-interdependency.md for the argument.

Convention: each edge asserts a direction of influence, never a magnitude. A signed walk multiplies asserted monotone hops (rung 2a); where influences on a node oppose, the net is refused (rung 2b, the O-3 straddle). The weighted read is a rendered counter-example (DEC-4), never a computed result.
