KEY Reading the schematic

Seven typed connectors make every claim walkable both directions to a named owner (G3); six gates are where the discipline bites. A gate firing is the exhibit, not a fault.

SHEET 1 The ASSAY apparatus — one heartbeat, whole

Read left to right: J-2 knowledge enters, passes (or is stopped at) its gate, compiles to a stamped world, scores to banded verdicts, and — because the R3m commitment set is infeasible — relaxes to a least-worst frontier before the commander selects. The delta recorder along the foot is the Bridge (S4), which watches and never writes.

What the drawing shows

One heartbeat of the demonstrator as an apparatus. Three J-2 inputs reach their gates: K9 passes clean; K8 is licensed by waiver W-1 (a waives line that travels with the value to wherever the north-approach constraint bites); K12 is contested, so the contest gate (G5) refuses compile; a malformed K10 write never becomes an object — the encoding lint refuses it at the door.

Downstream: compile stamps a world and feeds channels to score, whose four-stop verdicts carry margin bands. The R3m commitment set is infeasible, so the least-worst gate (G4) routes to relax — three inclusion-minimal cards, ranked, tie-break stated, never empty — before the commander selects.

Honesty, kept on the face of the drawing

Bands stay banded. Every assessed value is a gauge with lo–hi unit and its provenance chip; no bare assessed scalar appears. Gates are the exhibit. Each refusal is drawn as an honest outcome in its own styling, not hidden as an error.

Scripted where not yet computed. The staleness fan-out is marked scripted — thesis F is Stage 6, not-started; the drawing shows the walkthrough's oracle-consistent result and says so, per research note 07 §5. It will drive the real seam once Stage 6 lands.
